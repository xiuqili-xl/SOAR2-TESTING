title: Earth & Environmental Sciences Research Facilitator

bio: Julien Brun is the Earth and Environmental Sciences Research Facilitator at the UCSB Library. He is passionate about helping researchers in earth and environmental sciences make their work more reproducible by leveraging his data science skills to advise them on how to best manage their data and use programming tools to make their work more collaborative and reusable. Julien's scientific expertise is in ecohydrology (think vegetation - water interactions), Earth observation techniques (think satellite images 🛰️), and process-based modeling (think math 😅). He has taught numerous data science workshops, and he is also a lecturer for the data science program at the Bren School. On the personal side, Julien enjoys crunching chocolate, downhill skiing, mountain biking, and playing records on vintage Hi-Fi!
