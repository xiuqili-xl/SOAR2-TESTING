title: Director of Research Data Services
bio: Greg Janée is Director of Research Data Services at the UCSB Library.  He holds a BS in mathematics and an MS in computer science, both from UCSB. After a career developing research and commercial software, he joined UCSB to develop one of the first digital library systems, and researched how maps, remote sensing imagery, and other types of geospatial data could be made findable and accessible in new online environments. His interest in preserving the products of science led to joining the Library and creating the Research Data Services department, which helps researchers archive their data and has since expanded in scope to provide consultation and instruction in all areas of research computing and data. At work, Greg loves all things data and data science. Outside of work, he is an avid hiker and bicyclist and enjoys gardening and photography.
