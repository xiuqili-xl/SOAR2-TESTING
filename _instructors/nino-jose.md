title: Data Science Community Lead
bio: Jose Niño Muriel is the Data Science Community Lead at the UCSB Library. He holds a BA in Economics, a Master of Public Policy, and multiple certifications in data science and machine learning. His experience includes higher education teaching, economic consulting and research, and data analysis across the public and private sectors, including policy-oriented data analysis in governmental institutions. For the last two years, his role at UCSB has involved organizing and teaching workshops in partnership with The Carpentries community to teach foundational coding and data science skills to students and researchers. Outside of work, Jose loves beach walks with his wife and friends, cuddling with his cats, attending live sports or cultural events, and eating Colombian desserts like buñuelos and milhojas.
