title: Research and Engagement Librarian
bio: Anne Cong-Huyen (she/her) is a Research & Engagement Librarian at UCSB, where she works with the departments of Anthropology, Asian American Studies, Black Studies, Communication, and Global Studies. She holds a PhD in English from UCSB. Anne was previously at Columbia University Libraries where she was Director of Digital Scholarship Services (now Open Scholarship), and at the University of Michigan Library, where she was the Director of Digital Scholarship. At Michigan, she was also faculty in the Digital Studies Institute and Asian Pacific-Islander/American Studies. Anne is a principle of the SCRAM Collective, and was formerly a co-facilitator of FemTechNet. Anne has two dogs, two cats, and loves to eat and feed people. She is also a creative writer, who is currently at work on her first novel.
